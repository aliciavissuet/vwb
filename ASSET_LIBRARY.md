## Partner and institution marks

The files under `assets/timeline-partners/` are supporting editorial assets for specific timeline stories. They are not VWB-owned brand marks and should be used only when the related organization or collaboration is being discussed, subject to each organization's usage rules.

## Raw source material

Files beginning with `IMG_` that are JPEG photographs, plus the dated `Screenshot…` files, are source material. Review crop, resolution, consent, and context before using them publicly. The curated WebP files above are the cleaner first choice.

## Recommended social export package

For a complete reusable kit, produce each hero-logo treatment in:

- 1080 × 1080 PNG and MP4 — Instagram square
- 1080 × 1350 PNG and MP4 — Instagram portrait feed
- 1080 × 1920 PNG and MP4 — Stories and Reels
- Transparent SVG and PNG — presentations, print, and general design work
- GIF — presentation and messaging fallback only; use MP4 for Instagram
