From one-way to two-way exchange

A formal exchange was established between the UCSF Proctor Foundation and Chulalongkorn University—an annual relationship built around knowledge moving in both directions.

2021–22

Research translated into policy
2021–2022Federal policy

Changing corneal-donation rules

Research published in JAMA Ophthalmology showed that a five-year corneal-donation deferral lacked scientific basis. In 2021, more than 50 lawmakers cited the data in a bicameral letter; in 2022, the FDA changed its guidance.
