Who we are

Built to learn, act, and grow with purpose.

We begin with local needs and work alongside clinicians and communities to test solutions designed to last.

The organization is intentionally focused: prove the model carefully, strengthen it through partnership, and expand only what demonstrates value.

“Let’s get it done - and make it fun.”

About the founder

Blake Snyder, MD

Vision Without Borders was founded by Blake Snyder, MD, an ophthalmologist focused on expanding access to sight-restoring surgical care. The work is built around a simple premise: durable care starts with listening, showing up consistently, and working alongside local teams.

His clinical background informs the organization's practical approach: prove the model carefully, do the work well in one place, then scale with partners.
